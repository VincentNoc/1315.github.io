Topic: Technology in Healthcare, artificial body parts and organs.



Why we chose this topic?


The importance of artificial body parts rises, as the need for transplant organs and body parts outnumber the supply of available organs. Nowadays, thanks to the advancement in technology, it is possible to produce artificial body parts. Specially made for the individuals and this gives them a second chance of living a quality life.





What are artificial body parts?


In medicine, a prosthesis or prosthetic implant is an artificial device that replaces a missing body part, which may be lost through trauma, disease, or a condition present at birth.





So what is the purpose of artificial body parts and organs?


The purpose of artificial body parts and organs is to imitate the the movement and functionality of a real body part or limb. This allows people who have lost a body part or organ through, trauma, sickness or condition at birth to have that body part replaced.
